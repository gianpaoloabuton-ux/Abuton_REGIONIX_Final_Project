    • 3-Day Itinerary

    Day Morning Afternoon Evening
    Day 1 Arrive in Zamboanga City — check in and explore Paseo del Mar. Visit Fort Pilar Shrine and Museo de Zamboanga. Dinner by the seaside and sunset view at Paseo del Mar.
    Day 2 Island hopping at Great Sta. Cruz Island (Pink Sand Beach). Enjoy snorkeling or relaxing by the beach; picnic lunch. Return to city and have a seafood dinner at a local restaurant.
    Day 3 Visit Pasonanca Park or Abong-Abong Eco Park for a nature walk. Buy souvenirs at Barter Trade Center. Departure — safe travels!

    Travel Tips

    • Respect local culture — dress modestly and be courteous when visiting religious or heritage sites.
    • Learn basic Chavacano phrases like “Buenos días” (Good morning) or “Gracias” (Thank you).
    • Stay hydrated and wear sunscreen during outdoor activities.
    • Bring enough cash — some areas have limited ATM or card access.
    • Try local delicacies such as Curacha, Satti, and Zamboanga-style Paella.
    • Book transportation and accommodations early during festivals or peak season.
    • Visit between November and May for the best weather.
    • Help protect nature — don’t litter and avoid taking seashells or corals.
