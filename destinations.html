    • Fort Pillar, Zamboanga City

    Fort Pilar, also known as Real Fuerza de Nuestra Señora del Pilar, is a historic military defense fortress built in the 17th century in Zamboanga City. It stands as a symbol of the city's rich Spanish heritage and cultural history. Today, it serves as both a museum and a shrine, attracting visitors who come to explore its historical walls and pay devotion to Our Lady of the Pillar, the city's patroness.

    Rizal's Landing Place, Dapitan City

    Rizal's Landing Place in Dapitan City, Zamboanga del Norte, marks the historic site where Dr. José Rizal, the Philippine national hero, arrived in exile in 1892. The monument stands as a tribute to his time in Dapitan, where he shared his knowledge, served the community, and left a lasting legacy of education and service.

    Dipolog Sunset Boulevard, Dipolog City

    Dipolog Sunset Boulevard is one of the city's most popular attractions, known for its breathtaking sunset views along the Sulu Sea. It features a scenic coastal walkway lined with restaurants, shops, and recreational spaces, making it a perfect spot for relaxation, jogging, and enjoying the vibrant local atmosphere of Dipolog City.
